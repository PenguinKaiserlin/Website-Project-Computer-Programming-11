Overview of France

With its spectacular rural scenery, delectable food, and world-class wines, it's no surprise France is such a well-loved holiday destination. The country's diverse landscapes, from the winding Rhone to the rocky shores of Corsica, make it an unrivalled holiday destination, with so many reasons to keep returning year after year. Much like neighbouring Italy, it's a great holiday spot for anyone looking for a quintessential European holiday. You'll find plenty of beautiful places to visit, whether you're looking for an adventure or to simply relax.

Food in France

Anyone looking to book a foodie holiday will certainly consider France, a country famous for its food. From warm, crusty baguettes to hearty Boeuf Bourguignon and fresher-than-fresh seafood, there's so much to discover here for those who love to eat. Bordeaux is the viticulture capital of France, famous for deliciously full-bodied red wine, complemented by mouthwatering dishes rooted in tradition.

Landmarks in France

France is renowned for its iconic landmarks that reflect its rich history, art, and architectural beauty. The Eiffel Tower in Paris is the country’s most famous symbol, offering panoramic views of the capital. Nearby, the Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre Museum, home to the Mona Lisa, showcase Gothic grandeur and world-class art. The Palace of Versailles, just outside Paris, dazzles with its lavish halls and gardens. Beyond the capital, the Mont Saint-Michel rises dramatically off the Normandy coast, while the Pont du Gard and Château de Chambord highlight the elegance of Roman and Renaissance design. These landmarks, among many others, make France a dream destination for history and culture lovers.
